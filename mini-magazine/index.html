DIY Natural Bugspray Collar

Keep the bugs away the eco-friendly way By Darcy Matheson

Natural Bugspray Collar

MATERIALS

  • A bandana or collar made with natural fibers
  • 8–10 drops of a natural oil with bug-repelling properties, like cedar, geranium or lavender
  • 2 tbsp. of water

DIRECTIONS

Dilute the essential oil with a few tablespoons of water. Once mixed, use an eyedropper or turkey baster to apply to collar or bandana. Once dry, place on your dog’s neck. You can also rub a few drops of the diluted oil near the base of your dog’s tail.
